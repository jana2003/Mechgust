Who are we?


Chennai Institute of Technology


The Chennai Institute of Technology (CIT) was founded with the goal of offering quality technical education with enough industrial exposure, and it responds to the requirements of the young through creative teaching techniques. Mr. P. Sriram, the chairman, has been a successful first-generation entrepreneur in the metro for the past two decades, and his knowledge of the business fueled his desire to construct a technical educational institution under the name Chennai Institute of Technology. With a staff of committed academic members, this school delivers exceptional technical education. The campus is wired at 155 Mbps and wireless at 100 Mbps, with very complex technology and state-of-the-art laboratories. CIT has large and ventilated classrooms, labs with the most up-to-date high-tech equipment, and a digitalized central library with over 17000 volumes of books, National and International journals and magazines. Chennai Institute of Technology has played a significant role in promoting higher education for our students in technologically sophisticated countries. There are many students who want to study and work in other countries, and about 200 students are now enrolled in Japanese and German language classes.


CIT provides eight Undergraduate Courses (Mechanical, CSE, ECE, EEE, Civil, Mechatronics, Bio-Medical, IT, AI and Data science, Computer science and Business systems) and three Post Graduate Courses (CAD/CAM, CSE, and Applied Electronics). The institution thrusts on imparting the employability skill among the students in order to bridge the gap between the Institution and Industries and to equip the students with the current industrial skillset, CIT has established different training centers and Centre of Excellences such as, CIT – KUKA Industrial Robotics Training Centre - South India’s 1st Industrial Robot Training Centre in association with KUKA, Germany , CIT-Mobitics Mobile Centre of Excellence - India’s 1st Mobile Apps Development Centre , CIT – Accurate Calibration Centre - Centre of Excellence in CMM and Measurements, CIT Skill Development Centre affiliated to Automotive Skills Development Council (ASDC), Govt. of India, CIT – MK Auto Research Centre etc.


We are one of South India's top-rated universities for industry contacts and industry-collaborated activities. Our campus contains Technology Business Incubators, with close to 40 Incubant enterprises engaging in product development and industry consulting. CIT has worked with a number of international institutes, including Tianjin Vocational College of Mechanics and Electricity in China, RWTH Aachen University in Germany, Kyutech Institute of Technology in Japan, and Dongguk University in South Korea.


Mechanical Engineering


Mechanical engineering is the discipline that designs, analyzes, manufactures, and maintains mechanical systems using concepts from engineering, physics, engineering mathematics, and materials science. It is one of the oldest and most comprehensive engineering specialties. Mechanical engineering involves a solid grasp of fundamental concepts such as mechanics, dynamics, thermodynamics, materials science, structural analysis, and electricity. In addition to these fundamental principles, mechanical engineers design and analyze manufacturing plants, industrial equipment and machinery, heating and cooling systems, transportation systems, aircraft, watercraft, robotics, medical devices, weapons, and other items using tools such as computer-aided design (CAD), computer-aided manufacturing (CAM), and product life cycle management. It is the branch of engineering concerned with the design, manufacture, and operation of machines.
